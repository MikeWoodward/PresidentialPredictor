About this software

This software takes state-level opionion polls and makes a forecast of which party will win the US Presidential election. It produces state level forecasts and an Electoral College forecast.

It's based on the work of Sam Wang of the Princeton Election Consortium, but with some changes. The poll data comes from the Huffington Post Pollster API.
